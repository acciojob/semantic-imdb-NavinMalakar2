Summary

"Interstellar" is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie follows a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn in search of a new home for humanity as Earth faces environmental collapse. It blends emotion, science, and stunning visuals into a story about survival and love across time and space.

Actors

The film stars Matthew McConaughey as Cooper, Anne Hathaway as Dr. Brand, Jessica Chastain as Murph, and Michael Caine as Professor Brand.
